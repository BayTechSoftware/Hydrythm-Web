The discipline

Confidence before interpretation. Verification before intervention.

Before a reading changes your plans, ReefIQ checks it: is it fresh? did the source change? is the probe behaving normally? does an independent test agree? Only then does it interpret — against your tank's baseline — and recommend the safest next step.

Every important finding arrives as a Decision Card: the evidence, the uncertainty, the next step, and what not to change yet.
