What is ReefIQ?

ReefIQ is a mobile app for reef aquarium hobbyists. It brings your entire reef ecosystem into one workspace — wavemaker control, water parameter monitoring, tank history tracking, and an AI-powered reef assistant that understands your specific setup.

Connect your devices over local WiFi for real-time control and monitoring. Sign in to sync your tank profiles, parameter history, and assistant conversations across sessions. The ReefIQ Assistant provides personalized guidance based on your actual tank data and water chemistry.

Features

Wavemaker Control

Real-time speed, angle, and motion profile control over your local network. Multiple zone support with drag-to-reorder.

Parameter Dashboard

Live APEX probe readings and manual test logs on one dashboard. Daily snapshots, history charts, and health-range indicators.

Reef Assistant

Text and voice conversations with an AI that knows your tank — livestock, parameters, equipment, and history.

Over-the-Air Updates

Update your device firmware directly from the app. Verified integrity, one-tap install.

Integrations

  • Neptune APEX — Live probe readings (pH, ORP, temperature, salinity, alkalinity, calcium, magnesium, nitrate, phosphate) with automatic daily snapshots
  • ReefIQ FlowMaster — Stepper-driven wavemaker with encoder feedback and multi-zone profiles
  • ReefIQ Mini — Compact reef monitor with TFT display

Data & Permissions

  • Account: Email and display name when you sign in (supports Google Sign-In)
  • Tank data: Tank profiles, water parameters, and device configurations synced to your account
  • AI conversations: Text and voice messages processed for reef guidance
  • Microphone: Used for voice conversations with the Reef Assistant
  • Local network: Discovers and communicates with ReefIQ devices and APEX controllers on your WiFi
  • Bluetooth: Used for initial device setup and WiFi provisioning
