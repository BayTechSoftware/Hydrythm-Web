The ReefIQ Assistant & ReefIQ Cloud

The intelligence and infrastructure behind ReefIQ — designed, tuned, and secured for reef-keeping.

ReefIQ Assistant

Our own AI reef expert. It reads your live tank context — parameters, livestock, equipment, and journal — to give guidance specific to your reef, by text or natural voice. Advisory by design; you're always in control.

ReefIQ Cloud

Secure, encrypted sync for your tank data, history, insights, and notifications. Sign in once and your reef follows you across devices — protected in transit and at rest.
