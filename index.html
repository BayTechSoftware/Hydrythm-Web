About ReefIQ

ReefIQ is a reef aquarium management app that brings your entire ecosystem into one workspace. Monitor water parameters, control wavemakers, track tank history, and get AI-powered reef guidance — all from your phone.

🌊

Device Control

Real-time wavemaker control over local WiFi with live angle, speed, and profile management.

📊

Parameter Tracking

Automatic APEX readings and manual logs unified on one dashboard with history charts.

🤖

AI Reef Assistant

Text and voice conversations powered by AI, with full context of your tank data and parameters.

🔧

OTA Updates

Update device firmware over the air directly from the app with verified binary integrity.

Integrations

  • Neptune APEX — Live probe readings with automatic daily snapshots and mDNS hostname fallback
  • ReefIQ FlowMaster — Stepper-driven wavemaker with encoder feedback, multi-zone profiles
  • ReefIQ Mini — Compact reef monitor with TFT display
