Delete Your Account

You can delete your ReefIQ account and all associated data directly from within the app. No email or support request is needed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the ReefIQ app on your phone
  2. Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Authentication account — your Firebase login is permanently deleted
  • Tank profiles — all tank names, settings, and configurations
  • Water parameters — all stored readings (manual, Apex, device)
  • Livestock records — coral, fish, and invertebrate entries
  • Journal entries — all text and voice journal entries
  • AI Assistant history — all chat and voice conversation transcripts
  • Health reports — all AI-generated health assessments
  • ICP reports — uploaded PDFs and analysis results
  • Maintenance tasks — schedules and completion history
  • Parameter alerts — all configured alert rules
  • Device registrations — device links to your account
  • Device telemetry — all stored sensor readings
  • Push notification tokens — FCM registration

What Is NOT Deleted

  • Local app data — preferences stored on your device remain until you uninstall the app
  • Device firmware — your ReefIQ hardware devices (Flo, Cora, Specto) continue to operate independently

Additional Retention

No user data is retained after account deletion. The deletion is carried out via a GDPR-compliant cascade that removes all associated data from Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Realtime Database, and Firebase Storage.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, contact us at reefiq.tech@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account and we will process the deletion manually.
